How to Reach Us
Contact the developer via the Google Play Store listing page for Aura Guard using the "Email developer" or support contact option. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 30 days.

For users in the European Economic Area, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights under GDPR have been violated. However, as the Application processes no personal data remotely, such complaints would be more appropriately directed at device-level data handling.

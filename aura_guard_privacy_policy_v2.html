Overview

Aura Guard is an ambient sound monitoring and personal awareness application for Android. It uses on-device artificial intelligence to detect environmental sounds and voice patterns, alerting users through haptic, audio, and visual notifications.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The core principle of this Application is that all processing happens entirely on your device. We do not operate servers, collect personal information remotely, or display advertisements. No audio, voice data, or any derivative information is ever transmitted off your device.

✅
Our Privacy Commitment
Aura Guard does not collect, transmit, store remotely, sell, or share any personal data. All data created by the Application — including audio logs, detection records, and voice profiles — exists solely on your device and is fully under your control.
⚠️
Safety Notice NEW
Aura Guard is an assistive awareness tool and should not be relied upon as the sole method for personal safety, emergency detection, medical monitoring, or security-critical situations. It is designed to supplement, not replace, dedicated certified safety systems.
2
No Background Recording or Upload
NEW
🎙
Explicit Statement — No Recording Upload
Aura Guard does not continuously record or upload audio. Audio processing occurs transiently in memory for real-time detection only. Microphone audio is analysed frame-by-frame on-device and is never written to persistent storage in a continuous or full-session form. Only short, user-reviewable event snapshots are stored locally when a detection event occurs and logging is enabled.

What this means in practice:

  • The Application does not record conversations, ambient audio sessions, or full audio streams to storage.
  • Microphone access is used exclusively for real-time signal analysis. Audio samples are processed and then discarded from memory.
  • When a sound detection event occurs, a short audio snapshot of 3–10 seconds (depending on detection configuration) is saved locally so you can review what triggered the alert. This snapshot is never uploaded.
  • When the monitoring service is stopped, microphone access is released immediately. No audio capture occurs in the background while the service is off.
  • A persistent foreground notification is displayed at all times during monitoring, ensuring you are always aware the microphone is in use.

This design ensures Aura Guard functions as a real-time awareness tool, not a recording device.

Offline Operation
NEW
📶
Fully Offline — No Internet Required
Aura Guard functions entirely offline after installation. Internet access is not required for sound detection, voice matching, alert functionality, or any core feature of the Application. All artificial intelligence models, detection engines, and profile data operate locally on-device without any network connectivity.

This offline-first design means:

  • No audio, detection results, or profile data can be transmitted even if network connectivity is available — there is no code path in the Application to upload user data.
  • The Application works in airplane mode, in areas without network coverage, and on devices without a SIM card.
  • All AI models are bundled with the Application at install time and do not require network access to function.
ℹ️
A Note on Network Permissions
Although Android system libraries or bundled runtime components may include standard network-related declarations, Aura Guard itself does not transmit audio, voice data, detection logs, or any personal information to external servers. No network requests to any server are initiated by the Application's own code.

How We Use Information

All data created by the Application is used exclusively for the following on-device purposes:

  • Real-time sound detection — microphone audio is analysed frame-by-frame on-device to detect sounds matching your configured categories or enrolled profiles.
  • Alert triggering — when a sound is detected, the Application triggers your configured alert method (haptic, tone, passthrough, duck, or flash).
  • Detection logging — sound detection events are saved to the local Activity Log and Ambient Detection Log so you can review what the Application detected and when.
  • Profile matching — enrolled voice and sound profiles are compared against live audio on-device to determine whether a match has occurred.
  • User-driven improvement — you can review your detection logs to refine sensitivity settings and profile accuracy. This review happens entirely on your device.

The Application does not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, machine learning training on external servers, or any purpose other than the core sound detection functionality described above.

Sensitive Data & Biometric Information

Microphone / Audio Data

The Application requires continuous microphone access to function. Audio is processed in real-time on-device. When a detection event occurs and logging is enabled, a short audio snapshot of 3–10 seconds is saved locally alongside the detection record. This audio is stored only in the Application's private local storage and is never transmitted externally.

Biometric-Derived Data (Voice Identity Representations)

When you enroll a voice profile, the Application records audio samples, processes them through an on-device neural network, and produces a compact numerical representation of that voice. This numerical representation (not the raw audio) is stored locally. Source audio recordings used during enrollment are discarded immediately after the representation is computed.

🔐
Voice Data Use Limitation NEW
Voice identity representations are used solely for local matching on your device and are never used for identification across users, advertising, analytics, or profiling. Enrolled voice profiles exist only to trigger your configured alerts when your specified voice is detected nearby. They serve no other purpose.
⚠️
Important: Third-Party Voice Enrollment
You must obtain the explicit informed consent of any individual before enrolling their voice. Enrolling another person's voice without consent may violate applicable biometric privacy laws (including BIPA, GDPR, and equivalent legislation in your jurisdiction). The developer is not responsible for user compliance with applicable laws.

Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect locally stored data:

  • Android app sandboxing — all Application data is stored in the Application's private internal storage, inaccessible to other applications by Android's security model.
  • Device-level security — data is protected using Android application sandbox isolation and device-level security protections provided by the operating system, including full-disk or file-based encryption where enabled by your device and Android version. Aura Guard relies on these OS-level protections and does not apply additional application-layer encryption.
  • No remote attack surface — because no data is transmitted externally and the Application operates no servers, there is no remote attack surface through which data could be compromised.
  • Foreground service transparency — a persistent notification is displayed whenever the microphone is in use, ensuring you are always aware of monitoring activity.
  • Immediate microphone release — the microphone hardware resource is released as soon as the monitoring service is stopped, preventing any background audio capture.

Please note that the security of data stored on your device also depends on your own device security practices, including use of a screen lock, keeping your OS up to date, and preventing unauthorized physical access to your device.

ℹ️
Note on Rooted Devices
On devices where the Android security model has been modified (e.g. rooted devices), the app sandbox and OS-level encryption protections described above may not apply. We recommend not using the Application on rooted devices where biometric-derived profile data is enrolled.

Your Rights & Controls

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete, direct control over it at all times.

👁 Right to Access
View all detection log entries and enrolled profiles directly within the Application via the Ambient Log, Voice Activity Log, and Profile screens.
🗑 Right to Delete
Delete individual log entries, audio snapshots, and profiles from within the Application. Uninstalling deletes all data permanently.
✏️ Right to Correct
Rename enrolled profiles within the Application. Delete and re-enroll profiles to update or correct them.
⏹ Right to Withdraw Consent
Revoke microphone permission at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Aura Guard → Permissions. Monitoring stops immediately.
🔕 Right to Stop Processing
Stop the monitoring service at any time using the toggle on the main screen. The microphone is released immediately.
📦 Right to Portability
Detection log data is stored in SQLite databases within the Application's private directory. Standard Android backup tools may include this in device backups.

If you are located in a jurisdiction with additional privacy rights (such as the EU/EEA under GDPR, California under CCPA, or equivalent), you may contact us using the details in Section 14. As no data is held remotely, most requests can be fulfilled directly within the Application itself.
